Shampoo Recommendations

Choosing the right shampoo helps cleanse the scalp, remove buildup and maintain healthy hair. Different shampoos target concerns such as dandruff, hair fall and dryness.

Conditioner Recommendations

Conditioners help restore moisture, smooth hair strands and improve manageability. They are essential for maintaining soft and healthy looking hair.

Hair Oil Recommendations

Hair oils nourish the scalp, strengthen roots and help reduce hair breakage when used regularly. Traditional oils like onion oil and herbal blends are widely used for scalp health.

Hair Serum Recommendations

Hair serums help reduce frizz, add shine and protect hair from environmental damage. They are typically applied after washing to improve smoothness and manageability.

Hair Mask Recommendations

Hair masks provide deep nourishment and repair for dry or damaged hair. Weekly treatments help improve hair strength and overall texture.
